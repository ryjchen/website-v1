ABOUT

Hi, I'm Rebecca from Fountain Valley, CA.

I am problem solver and am interested in all things technology.

First and foremost, welcome to my design portfolio! I am currently a Master of Information Science student studying Human-Computer Interaction at University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. I love going out to experience new things, meet people, and am constantly looking for more things to learn and aquire skills in.

I am absolutely fascinated by the relationship between human and technology. During my time at UC Irvine, I completed an undergrad honors thesis on the perception of gravity in a rotating virtual environment and studied the effects of resulting cybersickness from the use of Oculus Rift. This experience really spurred my interest in pursing a career in UX / Product Design.
